  • Client: Indiana Department of Education
  • Project Date: July 2022 - Present
  • Project Site: https://indianagps.doe.in.gov
  • Tech Stack: Azure Analysis Service, ASP.NET MVC, PostgreSQL, PowerBI
  • Additional Information: This project was developed for and used by the Indiana Department of Education (IDOE). My role in this project was Senior Engineer for the ASP.NET MVC solution.
